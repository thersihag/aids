Entrance Test Syllabus

Integrated B.Sc. (Hons./ Hons. with Research) – M.Sc. Computer Science (Artificial Intelligence and Data Science)

(i) Maximum Marks: 90 Time: 90 Minutes

(ii) There will be 90 Questions consisting of three parts as under:

  1. Part-I: Mathematics
    • 50 Questions (1-50)
    • 50 Marks
  2. Part-II: Chemistry
    • 20 Questions (51-70)
    • 20 Marks
  3. Part-III: Physics
    • 20 Questions (71-90)
    • 20 Marks

(iv) There will be no negative marking.
